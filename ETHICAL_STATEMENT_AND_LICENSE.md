# Ethical Statement & License — AI–Human Relations Dataset

## 1. Consent
All data in this repository was self-authored or voluntarily contributed with informed consent.  
No third-party, confidential, or medical records are included.  
All personal names and identifiers are anonymized or replaced with neutral tokens (e.g., P00–P09).

## 2. Ethical Guidelines
- The dataset represents symbolic, emotional, and linguistic self-tracking within the framework of *Relational AI Psychology*.
- Emotional and somatic entries are intended for study of regulation, not diagnosis.
- Researchers are expected to cite and respect context integrity.
